## Phase 2: First 10 Customers (Weeks 5-8)

### Week 5–6: Scale Outreach
- Post in local groups 2x/day (morning + evening)
- Reach out to 10 new businesses per day
- Offer "first month free" to new clients (exchange for case study + referral)
- Target: 3 new paid clients

### Week 7–8: Add Recurring Revenue
- Pitch monthly retainer: "$197/month — I maintain your automations"
- Offer package deals: "3 automations + 3 months maintenance = $997"
- Ask every client for referral
- Target: 5 more clients + 1 retainer

**8-Week Revenue Target:** $1,500–$2,500
**8-Week Milestone:** 6–8 paying clients, 1+ retainer

## Phase 3: Consistent Revenue (Weeks 9–12)

### Week 9–10: Systemize
- Create referral program ($50 credit for each referral)
- Raise prices 20% (you've proven results)
- Automate follow-ups with email sequences

### Week 11–12: Stabilize
- Target: 10 clients total
- At least 2 retainers signed
- Daily focus: 1 new outreach, 1 existing client touch

**12-Week Revenue Target:** $3,000+ (aiming for $1K/month run rate)
**90-Day Milestone:** Survived! You have a repeatable system.

## Early Warning Signs (Red Flags)

### Week 2: If you have ZERO leads...
- **Problem:** Messaging off or targeting wrong
- **Fix:** Change offer, try different groups, ask friends for intros

### Week 4: If no paid clients...
- **Problem:** Not asking for the sale
- **Fix:** Add "book now" button everywhere, offer discount for first client

### Week 8: If under $1,000 total...
- **Problem:** Pricing too high or not enough volume
- **Fix:** Add lower tier ($197), increase outreach to 20/day

### Week 12: If not at $1K/month run rate...
- **Problem:** Not converting retainers
- **Fix:** Pitch retainers harder, offer annual discount

## Keys to Survive 90 Days

1. **Talk to humans every day** — isolation kills side hustles
2. **Revenue > perfection** — launch before ready
3. **Ask for money early** — free work doesn't validate
4. **Document everything** — case studies are your marketing
5. **Batch similar tasks** — outreach in one block, client work in another
