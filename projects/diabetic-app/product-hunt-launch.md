## Description

GLP1Companion is the first app designed specifically for GLP-1 medication users (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound).

Most health apps are built for diabetics. We built this for the 30% of Americans who will be on GLP-1s by 2030.

**Features:**
- 📊 Glucose & weight tracking
- 💉 Medication & injection logging
- 🤢 Side effect management (nausea, fatigue, hydration reminders)
- 📸 AI-powered food photos
- 🎤 Voice logging
- 📄 PDF reports for doctor visits
- 🧠 AI insights that actually help

**Pricing:**
- Free forever for basic tracking
- $12.99/month for AI insights, unlimited logs, and premium features
