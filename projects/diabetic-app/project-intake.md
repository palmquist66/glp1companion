# Project: GLP-1 Companion

**Date:** Feb 24, 2026

---

## 1. Project Basics

- **Project Name:** GLP-1 Companion
- **Type:** SaaS / Web App
- **One-line description:** Track GLP-1 medication usage, side effects, weight loss progress, and generate reports for patients

---

## 2. The "Why"

- **Problem it solves:** GLP-1 users (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound) need to track injections, side effects, weight, and share reports with doctors
- **Who has this problem:** GLP-1 medication users, weight loss clinics, diabetes patients
- **Why now:** GLP-1 market exploding 40% YoY, no dominant player yet

---

## 3. Goals & Success

- **Target outcome:** Passive income SaaS, eventually $300K/year
- **Revenue goal:** $1K/month → $10K/month → $300K/year
- **Timeline to first revenue:** Already built, needs monetization strategy
- **How will we measure success?** MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue), number of users
